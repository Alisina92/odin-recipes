Hotdog Recipe

Yummy Hotdog!

Hotdogs are made with frankforter sausages.
Hotdog first introduced to the USA by German immigrants.
Hotdogs can be eaten anytime of the day.

Ingredients:

  • Buns
  • Frankforter sausages
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps:

  1. Boil the water
  2. Add Franks to boiling water
  3. Toast the buns
  4. Put frankforter inside the bun
  5. Add the Ketchup and Mustard to the Sandwich
