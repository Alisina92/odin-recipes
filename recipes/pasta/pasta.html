Yummy Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a type of a Italian pasta.
That is famous in the world.
It is made with wheat flour and eaten with ragu sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti Pasta
  • Miced Meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Fry the miced meat
  2. Add to Tomato Sauce to it
  3. Add pasta to boling water
  4. Add the cooked Pasta to the sauce
  5. Serve with olive oil and cheese
