Pizza Recipe

Yummy Pizza!

Pizza is a famous Italian dish.

Pizza orignated from Naples when Queen Margaretia of Italy visited the City.

Pizza is loved by the world

Ingredients:

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Salami
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Spread tomato sauce on dough
  2. Add cheese on top
  3. Add Salami to the cheese
  4. Add more cheese on top
  5. Put in the oven for 15min over 200c
  6. Take it out and add olive oil to it
