Yummy Hotdog

Discription:

Hot Dog is a yummy street food orginally from Germany but developed in The United States.Hotdogs are made of frankforter sausages and served with buns.

Ingridients:

  • Sausages
  • Bread Buns
  • Onions
  • Mushrooms
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo

Steps:

  1. Boil Sausages
  2. Fry Onions and Mushrooms
  3. Melt the cheese over the Mushrooms
  4. Add the hotdog to the buns
  5. Add the Onion with Mushrooms and cheese to the hotdog
  6. Pour ketchup and Mayo in your sandwich
  7. Serve
